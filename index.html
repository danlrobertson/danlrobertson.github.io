About Me

I am interested in Software Engineering and "Type B" Data Science. I recently started as a Software Engineer at tripwire, and I also helped to start the Atlanta based non-profit Anidata. In my free time I enjoy contributing to open source projects, homebrewing, learning more about programming, fishing, and spending time with my lovely wife. My favorite language is Rust, but I spend most my time using C/C++ or Python. And yes, I use the best editor known to mankind... vim.
