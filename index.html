About Me

Hello! I'm Dan. I love fishing, contributing to open source projects, homebrewing, and hanging out with cattle. Despite my unique hobbies, many quirks, and interesting antics I somehow convinced my lovely wife to marry me. By day I am a Software Engineer at tripwire. I also recently helped to start the Atlanta based non-profit Anidata (Check us out on GitHub). And yes, I use the best editor known to mankind... vim.
