What can we learn from other OSS projects?

(heavily biased)
The culture of the organization is crucial to its success
  • Maintain an active "working directory"
  • Maintain an active chat channel
  • Maintain an active mailing list
  • Streamline the entry process
  • Standards
    • Define standards
    • Make them publicly available
    • Critique with care

Write some code!

Writing your first commit


  1. Make sure the project is active
  2. Research
    • Read the CONTRIBUTING.md
    • Read all the documentation you can
  3. Start small and find an easy issue

Final notes

  • Seek out help after you have done your research
  • Be very open to critiques.
